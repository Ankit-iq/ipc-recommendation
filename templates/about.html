About IPC Recommender

An AI-powered system that helps legal professionals, students, and researchers find relevant IPC sections using advanced natural language processing.

How It Works

Our system leverages state-of-the-art BERT (Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers) technology to understand the semantic meaning of your queries and match them with relevant IPC sections.

Natural Language Processing

Advanced NLP algorithms understand the context and meaning behind your legal queries, not just keyword matching.

Semantic Embeddings

BERT generates high-dimensional vector representations that capture the semantic relationships between your query and IPC sections.

Cosine Similarity

Measures the similarity between your query and each IPC section in vector space, providing accurate relevance scores.

Intelligent Ranking

Results are ranked by relevance score, ensuring the most applicable IPC sections appear at the top of your results.

Key Features

Intelligent Search

Enter natural language descriptions of legal scenarios and get relevant IPC sections instantly.

Relevance Scoring

Each recommendation comes with a confidence score showing how well it matches your query.

Comprehensive Database

Access the complete IPC with detailed descriptions and chapter classifications.

Responsive Design

Access the system from any device - desktop, tablet, or mobile phone.
